How It Works?

Embedded NFC Tags
Clothing items are equipped with small NFC tags or chips discreetly embedded within the fabric or attached to the garment.
These tags contain unique identifiers and store relevant information about the clothing item, such as brand, material, care instructions, purchase date, and even styling tips.
Tap and Access
To track and maintain your wardrobe, you simply tap your smartphone or NFC-enabled device on the NFC tag of the clothing item you wish to manage.
